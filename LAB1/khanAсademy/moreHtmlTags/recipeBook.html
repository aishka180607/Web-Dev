Recipe #1: Chocolate Sponge Cake

  • Time: 45 min
  • Serves: 6-8
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 120 g
Cocoa powder 40 g
Granulated sugar 150 g
Eggs 4
Unsalted butter (melted) 50 g
Milk 60 ml
Baking powder 1 tsp
Vanilla extract 1 tsp
Pinch of salt 1

Step 1: Preheat your oven to 180 degrees Celsius (356 degrees Fahrenheit). Grease and line a round cake tin with parchment paper.

Step 2: In a mixing bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and a pinch of salt.

Step 3: In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and sugar until the mixture is pale and fluffy. Add vanilla extract and mix well.

Step 4: Gradually fold the dry ingredients into the egg mixture, alternating with milk and melted butter. Mix gently until just combined.

Step 5: Pour the batter into the prepared cake tin, spreading it evenly. Tap the tin lightly to remove air bubbles.

Step 6: Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

Step 7: Allow the cake to cool in the tin for 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely. Decorate or serve as desired.
